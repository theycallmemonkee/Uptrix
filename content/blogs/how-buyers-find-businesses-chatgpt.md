## Optimizing for Generative Engine Recommendations

GEO (Generative Engine Optimization) is the new SEO. When buyers ask ChatGPT or Perplexity for solutions, recommendations depend on structured citations.

### Optimization Action Steps

Optimize your technical schema tags, secure authority references in niche media, and publish in depth solutions addressing specific conversational search queries.

takeaway: If AI engines cannot find structured citations of your brand, your business does not exist for modern buyers.
