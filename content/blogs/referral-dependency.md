## The Danger of Word of Mouth

Relying entirely on referrals means having no control over pipeline volume or timing. When referrals dry up, the revenue pipeline collapses.

### Transitioning to an Inbound Engine

An inbound demand generation system establishes a monthly floor of enquiries. By linking paid ads, SEO, and lead capture, you build an acquisition engine that works independently of luck.

takeaway: Referrals are a growth multiplier, but a unified demand generation system is the growth foundation.
