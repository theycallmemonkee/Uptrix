## Organic Compounding vs Immediate Paid Scaling

SEO builds long term equity, reducing your cost per lead over time. Paid ads generate instant pipeline volume. 

### Why You Need Both in Unification

Running them in isolation leads to traffic leaks or high customer acquisition costs. By combining ads with SEO, organic search compounds while paid media covers initial gaps.

takeaway: Do not choose between SEO and ads. Connect them into a single demand system where traffic feeds conversions.
