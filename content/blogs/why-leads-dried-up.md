## The Shift to AI-Powered Search

Traditional SEO focused on keywords and click-through rates. Today, buyers are asking direct questions in AI engines like ChatGPT, Perplexity, and Gemini.

### How AI Search Recommends Brands

AI engines do not just index links; they synthesize recommendations based on authority, citations, and product reviews. To get found, your brand must be cited inside AI databases.

takeaway: Visibility in AI search requires structured schema, authority content, and optimization for conversational intent.
